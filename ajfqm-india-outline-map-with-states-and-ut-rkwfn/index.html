Introduction

India is a vast country with diverse cultures and traditions. To understand the geography of India, it is important to know about the states and union territories that make up the country. An outline map of India helps in understanding the location of these states and UTs.

The States of India

India is divided into 28 states and 8 union territories. The states are Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Odisha, Punjab, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and West Bengal.

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is located in the southeastern part of India. The capital of Andhra Pradesh is Amaravati, and the largest city is Visakhapatnam. It is known for its coastline, temples, and scenic beauty.

Bihar

Bihar is located in the eastern part of India. The capital of Bihar is Patna, and the largest city is Patna. It is known for its rich history, culture, and heritage.

Gujarat

Gujarat is located in the western part of India. The capital of Gujarat is Gandhinagar, and the largest city is Ahmedabad. It is known for its vibrant culture, rich heritage, and scenic beauty.

Kerala

Kerala is located in the southwestern part of India. The capital of Kerala is Thiruvananthapuram, and the largest city is Kochi. It is known for its backwaters, beaches, and scenic beauty.

Union Territories of India

India has 8 union territories. They are Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Chandigarh, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, Lakshadweep, Delhi, Puducherry, Jammu and Kashmir, and Ladakh.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Andaman and Nicobar Islands are located in the Bay of Bengal. The capital of Andaman and Nicobar Islands is Port Blair. It is known for its beaches, coral reefs, and pristine beauty.

Delhi

Delhi is a union territory and the capital of India. It is known for its rich history, culture, and heritage. It is also the political center of India.

Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a union territory located in the northern part of India. The capital of Jammu and Kashmir is Srinagar. It is known for its scenic beauty, snow-capped mountains, and rich culture.

Conclusion

An outline map of India with states and UTs is an essential tool for understanding the geography of India. It helps in identifying the location of different states and UTs and their unique features. Knowing about the states and UTs of India is important for anyone interested in Indian culture, history, or politics.
