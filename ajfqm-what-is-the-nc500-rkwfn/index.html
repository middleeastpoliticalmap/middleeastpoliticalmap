What Is The Nc500

November 4, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Are you looking for a Scottish adventure that will take your breath away? Look no further than the North Coast 500 (NC500), a 500-mile route that takes you through the most stunning landscapes in Scotland. In this article, we will explore what the NC500 is and why it has become such a popular destination among road trippers.

The Route

The NC500 starts and ends in Inverness, taking you through the Scottish Highlands, along the rugged coastline, and past ancient castles and charming villages. The route can be completed in five to seven days, depending on how much time you want to spend exploring each area.

Day 1: Inverness to Dornoch

Your journey begins in Inverness, a city steeped in history and charm. From there, you will head north to the quaint town of Dornoch, where you can explore the stunning Dornoch Cathedral and enjoy a round of golf at the Royal Dornoch Golf Club.

Day 2: Dornoch to John o’ Groats

On day two, you will head to John o’ Groats, the northernmost point on the British mainland. Along the way, you will pass through the picturesque villages of Wick and Lybster, where you can take in the stunning coastal views.

Day 3: John o’ Groats to Ullapool

Day three takes you through the dramatic landscapes of Caithness and Sutherland, where you can explore the stunning beaches and quaint villages. Your journey ends in Ullapool, a charming fishing village that offers stunning views of Loch Broom.

Day 4: Ullapool to Applecross

On day four, you will drive through the majestic Torridon Mountains, where you can take in the stunning views and explore the ancient villages of Shieldaig and Applecross.

Day 5: Applecross to Inverness

On your final day, you will head back to Inverness, passing through the charming villages of Lochcarron and Beauly. Make sure to stop at the iconic Eilean Donan Castle, which is perched on a small island at the meeting point of three lochs.

Why NC500 Is So Popular

The NC500 has become a popular destination among road trippers because it offers a unique blend of stunning landscapes, charming villages, and ancient history. The route takes you through some of the most remote and untouched areas of Scotland, where you can experience the true beauty of the country. In addition, the route is easy to navigate and offers plenty of opportunities for rest and relaxation.

Tips for a Successful NC500 Trip

If you’re planning a road trip on the NC500, here are some tips to make your journey a success:

  • Start planning early to ensure you have enough time to see everything you want to see.
  • Make sure your car is in good condition and bring a spare tire just in case.
  • Bring warm clothing, as the weather in Scotland can be unpredictable.
  • Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak season.
  • Stock up on snacks and drinks, as there are not many options for food along the route.

Conclusion

The NC500 is a must-see destination for anyone looking to explore the stunning landscapes of Scotland. With its rugged coastline, majestic mountains, and ancient castles, the route offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rich history. So pack your bags, hop in your car, and get ready for the adventure of a lifetime!
