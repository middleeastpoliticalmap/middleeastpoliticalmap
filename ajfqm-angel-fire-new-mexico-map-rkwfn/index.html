Angel Fire New Mexico Map

July 16, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Are you planning to visit Angel Fire, New Mexico? If yes, then it’s a great decision! This beautiful location offers breathtaking scenic views, outdoor activities, and a rich cultural experience. And to make the most of your visit, you must start by exploring the Angel Fire New Mexico map. In this article, we will guide you through the must-visit spots in Angel Fire, New Mexico, through its map.

1. Angel Fire Resort

Angel Fire Resort is the heart of Angel Fire, New Mexico. It’s a perfect place for skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports. The resort has over 80 ski trails, 7 lifts, and a terrain park. If you’re not interested in skiing, you can still enjoy the scenic beauty of the resort by taking the scenic chairlift or a sleigh ride.

2. Vietnam Veterans Memorial State Park

The Vietnam Veterans Memorial State Park is a must-visit spot for history buffs. It’s a 1.5-acre park that honors the New Mexicans who served in the Vietnam War. The park has a chapel, a museum, and a replica of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Wall. The park is open year-round, and admission is free.

3. Enchanted Circle Drive

One of the most beautiful drives in the world is the Enchanted Circle Drive. It’s a 83-mile scenic drive that takes you through mountains, forests, and valleys. The drive starts and ends in Taos, and it passes through Angel Fire, Red River, and Eagle Nest. The drive is open year-round, but it’s best to visit during fall when the leaves change color.

4. Eagle Nest Lake State Park

Eagle Nest Lake State Park is a popular spot for fishing, boating, and camping. The lake is stocked with rainbow trout, and you can catch other fish like kokanee salmon, yellow perch, and northern pike. The park has a campground, picnic areas, and hiking trails. It’s open year-round, but the best time to visit is during summer.

5. Angel Fire Bike Park

Angel Fire Bike Park is a paradise for mountain bikers. The park has over 60 miles of trails that cater to all skill levels. The trails are designed by professional riders, and they offer a thrilling experience to bikers. The park also has a bike rental facility, a repair shop, and a restaurant.

6. Monte Verde Lake

Monte Verde Lake is a serene spot for fishing and picnicking. The lake is stocked with rainbow trout, and you can catch other fish like brown trout, cutthroat trout, and kokanee salmon. The lake has picnic tables, grills, and a playground. It’s open year-round, but the best time to visit is during summer.

7. Angel Fire Art + Farmers’ Market

The Angel Fire Art + Farmers’ Market is a weekly event that takes place during summer. The market features local artists, craftsmen, and farmers. You can find handmade crafts, fresh produce, and delicious food at the market. The market also has live music, and it’s a great place to socialize with locals.

8. Angel Fire Community Center

The Angel Fire Community Center is a hub for social and recreational activities. The center has a fitness center, a pool, a hot tub, and a sauna. It also hosts events like yoga classes, art classes, and movie nights. The center is open year-round, and it’s a great place to stay active and meet new people.

9. Angel Fire Airport

Angel Fire Airport is a small airport that serves private and charter flights. The airport has a 8,900-foot runway, and it’s located at an elevation of 8,382 feet. The airport offers stunning views of the mountains, and it’s a great place for aviation enthusiasts.

10. Angel Fire Chamber of Commerce

The Angel Fire Chamber of Commerce is a great resource for visitors. The chamber has information about local businesses, events, and attractions. It also offers maps, brochures, and coupons. The chamber is open year-round, and it’s a great place to start your Angel Fire adventure. In conclusion, Angel Fire, New Mexico, is a beautiful location that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re interested in skiing, fishing, mountain biking, or just relaxing, you’ll find it all in Angel Fire. And the best way to explore Angel Fire is through its map. So, grab a map, plan your itinerary, and get ready for an unforgettable experience!
