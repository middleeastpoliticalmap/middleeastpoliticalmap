How To Print Directions On Mapquest

December 11, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

In this day and age, with all the mapping apps and GPS devices available, it’s easy to forget that sometimes we still need to print out directions. MapQuest is one of the oldest and most reliable mapping websites, and it’s still a go-to for many people. Here’s how to print directions on MapQuest in 2023.

Step 1: Visit the MapQuest Website

The first step is to visit the MapQuest website. You can do this by typing “mapquest.com” into your web browser’s address bar, or by searching for “MapQuest” on your favorite search engine. Once you’re on the website, you’ll see a search bar where you can enter your starting address and destination.

Step 2: Enter Your Starting Address and Destination

Enter your starting address and destination into the search bar. You can also enter additional stops or waypoints if you need to make any detours along the way. Make sure to double-check your addresses before moving on to the next step.

Step 3: Review Your Route

Once you’ve entered your addresses, MapQuest will generate a route for you. Take a moment to review the route and make sure it looks correct. If you need to make any changes, you can click and drag the route to adjust it.

Step 4: Click the “Print” Button

When you’re satisfied with your route, click the “Print” button located at the top-right corner of the page. This will open the print preview window.

Step 5: Adjust Your Print Settings

Before you print your directions, you may need to adjust your print settings. You can choose to print in color or black and white, adjust the size of the print, and choose whether or not to include a map of your route. Make sure to select the options that work best for you.

Step 6: Print Your Directions

Once you’ve adjusted your print settings, click the “Print” button to print your directions. You can choose to print to a printer connected to your computer, or to a PDF file if you prefer to save your directions digitally.

Step 7: Follow Your Directions

Now that you’ve printed your directions, you’re ready to hit the road! Make sure to follow the directions carefully and stay safe on your journey.

Tips for Printing Directions on MapQuest

– Double-check your addresses before printing to make sure they’re accurate. – Adjust your print settings to get the best results. – Consider printing a map of your route if you’re not familiar with the area. – If you have a long route, consider breaking it up into smaller sections to make it easier to read. – Always follow traffic laws and stay safe on the road.

Conclusion

Printing directions on MapQuest is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. By following these tips and tricks, you can ensure that you have accurate directions and a safe journey. Happy travels!
