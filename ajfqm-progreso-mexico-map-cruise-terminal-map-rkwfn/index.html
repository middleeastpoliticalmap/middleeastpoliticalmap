Progreso Mexico Map Cruise Terminal Map

Introduction

If you’re planning to go on a cruise to Mexico, chances are you’ll be stopping at the port of Progreso. This small but charming town is located on the Yucatan Peninsula, just a few miles from the city of Merida. In this article, we’ll take a look at the Progreso, Mexico map and explore the cruise terminal area.

The Progreso Cruise Terminal

The Progreso cruise terminal is located just a few steps away from the town’s main square. It’s a small but efficient terminal that can handle up to two ships at a time. The terminal has all the necessary facilities, including a duty-free shop, a bar, and a restaurant.

The Progreso, Mexico Map

The Progreso, Mexico map is a useful tool for anyone visiting the town. The map shows the location of the cruise terminal, as well as the main attractions in the area. You can easily find your way around town using the map, which is available at the terminal or online.

Getting Around Progreso

Progreso is a small town that can easily be explored on foot. However, if you want to venture further afield, you can rent a car or take a taxi. There are also tours available that will take you to nearby attractions such as the Mayan ruins of Chichen Itza or the city of Merida.

Things to Do in Progreso

Progreso may be a small town, but there are plenty of things to see and do. Here are some of the top attractions:

The Malecon

The Malecon is Progreso’s main promenade, located just a few steps from the cruise terminal. It’s a great place to take a stroll, enjoy the ocean breeze, and watch the locals go about their day.

The Beach

Progreso has a long, sandy beach that’s perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing. There are plenty of beach bars and restaurants where you can grab a cold drink or a bite to eat.

The Market

The market is located just a few blocks from the cruise terminal and is a great place to shop for souvenirs, handicrafts, and local products. You can find everything from handmade jewelry to traditional Mexican clothing.

Conclusion

Progreso, Mexico, may be a small town, but it has plenty to offer visitors. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing day at the beach or an adventure exploring nearby attractions, Progreso has something for everyone. Be sure to check out the Progreso, Mexico map and plan your visit accordingly.
