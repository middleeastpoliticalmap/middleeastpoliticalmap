What is QEEG Brain Mapping?

QEEG (Quantitative Electroencephalography) Brain Mapping is a non-invasive diagnostic procedure that measures the electrical activity in the brain. This procedure involves the placement of sensors on the scalp, which record the brainwaves and provide information on the brain’s functioning.

Why is QEEG Brain Mapping Important?

QEEG Brain Mapping is important because it can help diagnose a range of neurological and psychological disorders, including ADHD, depression, anxiety, and traumatic brain injuries. It can also be used to track changes in brain activity over time and monitor the effectiveness of treatments.

How Does QEEG Brain Mapping Work?

During a QEEG Brain Mapping session, the patient sits in a comfortable chair while the sensors are placed on their scalp. The sensors record the brainwaves while the patient performs various tasks, such as relaxing, focusing, and closing their eyes. The data collected is then analyzed by a qualified neurologist or psychologist, who can interpret the results and make a diagnosis.

Who Can Benefit from QEEG Brain Mapping?

QEEG Brain Mapping can benefit anyone who is experiencing neurological or psychological symptoms, including children, adults, and seniors. It can also be used as a preventative measure for those who are at risk of developing certain disorders.

What Should I Expect During a QEEG Brain Mapping Session?

During a QEEG Brain Mapping session, you can expect to sit comfortably in a chair while the sensors are placed on your scalp. You will be asked to perform various tasks, such as relaxing, focusing, and closing your eyes. What Are the Benefits of QEEG Brain Mapping?

The benefits of QEEG Brain Mapping include accurate diagnosis of neurological and psychological disorders, monitoring of treatment effectiveness, and early detection of potential problems. It can also provide valuable information on brain functioning, which can be used to improve cognitive performance and enhance overall well-being.

Are There Any Risks or Side Effects of QEEG Brain Mapping?

QEEG Brain Mapping is a non-invasive procedure that does not involve any radiation or harmful side effects. However, some patients may experience mild discomfort or irritation from the sensors placed on their scalp.
