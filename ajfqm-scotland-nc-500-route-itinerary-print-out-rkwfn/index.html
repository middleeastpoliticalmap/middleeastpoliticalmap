Scotland Nc 500 Route Itinerary Print Out

November 16, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction: Exploring the Scotland NC 500 Route

Are you planning a road trip to Scotland and looking for the ultimate itinerary guide? Look no further than the NC 500 route. This 500-mile route takes you through the stunning Scottish Highlands, from Inverness to John o’ Groats and back down to the west coast. With breathtaking landscapes, charming villages, and historic landmarks, the Scotland NC 500 route is a must-see for any traveler.

Getting Started: Preparing for Your Journey

Before hitting the road, it’s important to plan your itinerary. You can find plenty of resources online, including printable maps and route guides. We recommend printing out a detailed itinerary, including the places you want to visit, the distances between them, and any important information like opening times and admission fees.

Day 1-2: Inverness and the Black Isle

Start your journey in Inverness, the capital of the Highlands. Take a stroll along the River Ness, visit the Inverness Castle, and explore the Victorian Market. From there, head to the Black Isle, known for its stunning coastline and wildlife. Don’t miss the Chanonry Point lighthouse, where you can spot dolphins from the shore.

Day 3-4: West Coast Adventure

Next, head west to the charming fishing village of Ullapool. Take a boat trip to the Summer Isles, hike the stunning mountains, or simply relax and enjoy the local seafood. From there, drive along the rugged coastline to the famous Eilean Donan Castle, which has been featured in many films and TV shows.

Day 5-6: The North Coast

The North Coast is the most scenic part of the NC 500 route, with stunning beaches, rugged cliffs, and charming villages. Stop at the Smoo Cave, where you can take a boat trip into the heart of the cave, or visit the historic village of Durness, where you can learn about the area’s rich history.

Day 7-8: John o’ Groats and the East Coast

No trip to Scotland is complete without a visit to John o’ Groats, the northernmost point of mainland Britain. Take a photo at the famous signpost and explore the nearby villages. From there, head south along the east coast, where you can visit the stunning Dunrobin Castle, the picturesque fishing village of Portsoy, and the historic town of Elgin.

Tips for a Successful Road Trip

While the Scotland NC 500 route is a stunning journey, it’s important to be prepared for the challenges of the road. Make sure to pack extra food and water, as well as warm clothing and a first aid kit. Be prepared for narrow roads and steep hills, and take your time to enjoy the stunning scenery.

Conclusion: The Ultimate Scotland NC 500 Route Itinerary

The Scotland NC 500 route is a stunning journey that takes you through some of the most beautiful landscapes in the world. With this itinerary guide, you can make the most of your trip and experience the best of what Scotland has to offer. So pack your bags, hit the road, and get ready for an adventure of a lifetime.
