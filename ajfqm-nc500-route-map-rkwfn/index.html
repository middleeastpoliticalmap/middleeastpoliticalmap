Nc500 Route Map

March 22, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Are you looking for an adventurous road trip in the Scottish Highlands? Look no further than the NC500 route map. This scenic drive takes you through stunning landscapes, charming villages, and historic castles. In this article, we will guide you through the NC500 route map and provide you with tips on how to make the most of your journey.

What is NC500?

NC500 stands for North Coast 500, a 500-mile route that starts and ends at Inverness Castle. The route takes you through the north-western coast of Scotland, covering the Highlands and parts of the North Coast. The NC500 route map has become increasingly popular among tourists who want to explore the hidden gems of Scotland.

The Route

The NC500 route map starts at Inverness Castle and takes you through the Black Isle, Caithness, Sutherland, and Wester Ross. You will pass through some of the most scenic landscapes in Scotland, including rugged coastlines, rolling hills, and stunning lochs. The route also takes you through charming villages, where you can stop for a coffee or a bite to eat.

What to See and Do

The NC500 route map offers plenty of attractions and activities for all types of travelers. Here are some of the must-see spots:

Dunrobin Castle

This historic castle is one of the most popular attractions on the NC500 route map. The castle has a stunning architecture, a beautiful garden, and a falconry display that is sure to impress.

John O’Groats

This small village is famous for being the most northerly point on the British mainland. It’s a great spot to take a photo and grab a souvenir.

Smoo Cave

This unique cave is located near Durness and is known for its dramatic entrance and stunning waterfall. You can take a guided tour of the cave and learn about its history and geology.

Where to Stay

The NC500 route map has plenty of accommodation options, ranging from campsites to luxury hotels. Here are some of our top picks:

Glencanisp Lodge

This luxury lodge is located in Assynt, offering stunning views of the Scottish Highlands. The lodge has comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and plenty of outdoor activities.

Sands Caravan and Camping

This family-friendly campsite is located near Gairloch, offering stunning views of the coast. The site has spacious pitches, hot showers, and a playground for kids.

Tips for Driving the NC500 Route Map

Driving the NC500 route map can be challenging, especially for inexperienced drivers. Here are some tips to make your journey smoother:

Plan your Itinerary

Make sure to plan your itinerary in advance, including your stops and accommodation. This will help you avoid last-minute stress and ensure that you make the most of your journey.

Drive Carefully

The NC500 route map has plenty of narrow and winding roads, so make sure to drive carefully and follow the speed limits. Be prepared for sudden weather changes and wildlife on the road.

Take Your Time

The NC500 route map is all about enjoying the journey, so don’t rush it. Take your time to stop and appreciate the stunning landscapes, charming villages, and historic sites.

Conclusion

The NC500 route map is a must-do road trip for anyone who wants to explore the Scottish Highlands. With stunning landscapes, charming villages, and historic sites, this route has something for everyone. Follow our tips and make the most of your journey.
