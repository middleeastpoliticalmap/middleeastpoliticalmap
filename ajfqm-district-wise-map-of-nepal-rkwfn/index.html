District Wise Map Of Nepal

October 8, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Nepal, a landlocked country in South Asia, is home to diverse topography, cultures, and traditions. The country is divided into 7 provinces, 77 districts, and 753 local units. Each district in Nepal has its unique identity, history, and geography. In this article, we will explore the district wise map of Nepal and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Geography of Nepal

Before we dive into the district wise map of Nepal, let’s take a look at the country’s geography. Nepal is located in the Himalayas and is home to the world’s highest peak, Mount Everest. The country is divided into three geographical regions: the Himalayas, the Hill Region, and the Terai Region. Each region has a distinct climate, vegetation, and culture.

Provinces of Nepal

Nepal is divided into seven provinces, which are further divided into districts. The provinces are named after their headquarters and are as follows:

Province 1:

Province 1 is located in the eastern part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Biratnagar, Dharan, and Itahari. The province is divided into 14 districts.

Province 2:

Province 2 is located in the southern part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Janakpur, Birgunj, and Bardibas. The province is divided into eight districts.

Province 3:

Province 3 is located in the central part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Kathmandu, Lalitpur, and Bhaktapur. The province is divided into 13 districts.

Province 4:

Province 4 is located in the western part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Pokhara and Baglung. The province is divided into 11 districts.

Province 5:

Province 5 is located in the mid-western part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Butwal, Tansen, and Nepalgunj. The province is divided into 12 districts.

Province 6:

Province 6 is located in the northwestern part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Surkhet, Dailekh, and Jumla. The province is divided into 10 districts.

Province 7:

Province 7 is located in the far-western part of Nepal and is home to major cities like Dhangadhi and Mahendranagar. The province is divided into nine districts.

Districts of Nepal

Nepal is divided into 77 districts, each with its unique identity, history, and geography. The districts are further divided into local units, which are responsible for providing basic services to the people. Some of the major districts in Nepal are:

Kathmandu:

Kathmandu is the capital city of Nepal and is located in Province 3. It is known for its rich history, culture, and architecture. The city is home to major tourist attractions like Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and Swayambhunath Stupa.

Pokhara:

Pokhara is a popular tourist destination in Nepal and is located in Province 4. It is known for its natural beauty, including lakes, waterfalls, and mountains. The city is also a popular destination for adventure sports like paragliding and trekking.

Chitwan:

Chitwan is a district in Province 3 and is known for its national park. The Chitwan National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to various species of flora and fauna, including the Bengal tiger and the one-horned rhinoceros.

Jhapa:

Jhapa is a district in Province 1 and is located in the southern part of Nepal. It is known for its tea gardens and is home to major tourist attractions like Kechana Kalan and Gokarna Mahadev Temple.

Conclusion

The district wise map of Nepal provides us with a comprehensive guide to the country’s geography, culture, and history. Each district in Nepal has its unique identity, and exploring them can be a fulfilling experience. Whether you are a tourist or a local, understanding the district wise map of Nepal can help you navigate through the country and appreciate its diversity.
