Jacksonville Nc Beaches Map

March 27, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Jacksonville, North Carolina, is home to some of the most beautiful beaches on the East Coast. With its mild climate, crystal-clear waters, and beautiful scenery, it’s no wonder that people flock to this area every year. If you’re planning a trip to Jacksonville, you’ll want to know about the best beaches in the area. In this article, we’ll take a look at the Jacksonville NC beaches map and give you a guide to the best beaches in the area.

Top Beaches in Jacksonville

1. Onslow Beach

Onslow Beach is located on Camp Lejeune and is open to the public. This beach has everything you need for a day of fun in the sun, including lifeguards, restrooms, showers, and picnic areas. Onslow Beach is also home to a campground, so you can stay overnight and enjoy the beach even longer.

2. North Topsail Beach

North Topsail Beach is a great place to go if you’re looking for a quieter beach experience. This beach is located on Topsail Island and is known for its beautiful sunrises and sunsets. North Topsail Beach is also home to a sea turtle sanctuary, so you may even spot some baby turtles during your visit.

3. Surf City Beach

Surf City Beach is located on Topsail Island and is a popular spot for surfing. This beach has a wide range of amenities, including restrooms, showers, and picnic areas. Surf City Beach is also home to a fishing pier, so you can try your luck at catching some fish while you’re there.

Other Beaches in the Area

1. Emerald Isle Beach

Emerald Isle Beach is located about an hour from Jacksonville and is a great place to go if you’re looking for a more secluded beach experience. This beach is known for its clear waters and pristine sand.

2. Atlantic Beach

Atlantic Beach is located about 45 minutes from Jacksonville and is a popular spot for families. This beach has a wide range of amenities, including restrooms, showers, and picnic areas. Atlantic Beach is also home to a fishing pier and a boardwalk.

3. Wrightsville Beach

Wrightsville Beach is located about an hour and a half from Jacksonville and is a popular spot for surfing. This beach has a wide range of amenities, including restrooms, showers, and picnic areas. Wrightsville Beach is also home to a fishing pier and a boardwalk.

Tips for Visiting the Beaches in Jacksonville

When visiting the beaches in Jacksonville, there are a few tips you should keep in mind. First, always check the weather before you go. You don’t want to get caught in a storm while you’re at the beach. Second, make sure you pack plenty of sunscreen and water. The sun can be intense, and it’s important to stay hydrated. Finally, be respectful of the environment. Don’t litter, and make sure you clean up after yourself.

Conclusion

Jacksonville NC beaches map offers a range of beautiful beaches for visitors to enjoy. From Onslow Beach to North Topsail Beach, there’s something for everyone in this area. Whether you’re looking for a quiet beach experience or a place to go surfing, you’re sure to find it in Jacksonville. So pack your bags, grab your sunscreen, and head to the beach for a day of fun in the sun.
