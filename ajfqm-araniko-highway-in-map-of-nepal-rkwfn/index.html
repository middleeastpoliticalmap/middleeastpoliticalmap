Araniko Highway In Map Of Nepal

Introduction

Located in the north-eastern region of Nepal, Araniko Highway is one of the most scenic highways in the country. The highway is named after Araniko, a famous Nepalese artist and architect who was born in the region. The highway connects Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, with the border town of Kodari, which is the gateway to Tibet. The highway is a major trade route between Nepal and China.

The History of Araniko Highway

Araniko Highway was built in the 1960s with the assistance of the Chinese government. The highway was constructed to provide a direct link between Nepal and China, which was previously only accessible by foot. The highway was named after Araniko, who had traveled to China in the 13th century and had contributed to the construction of many important buildings in China.

The Route

The highway is approximately 114 kilometers long and takes around 5 hours to drive. The route is full of beautiful scenery, including snow-capped mountains, rivers, and forests. The highway passes through several towns and villages, including Dhulikhel, Banepa, and Barhabise.

Challenges of Araniko Highway

The highway is located in a geologically unstable region and is prone to landslides and roadblocks, especially during the monsoon season. The region is also prone to earthquakes, which can cause significant damage to the highway. Despite these challenges, the highway remains an important trade and transportation route in the region.

The Importance of Araniko Highway

Araniko Highway is a vital trade route between Nepal and China. The highway is used to transport goods and supplies between the two countries, including food, fuel, and construction materials. The highway is also an important transportation route for tourists who want to visit Tibet or the Everest region of Nepal.

The Future of Araniko Highway

The Nepalese government has plans to upgrade and expand Araniko Highway in the coming years. The government plans to widen the highway and improve its infrastructure to make it safer and more efficient. The government also plans to construct a railway line along the highway to improve transportation and trade between Nepal and China.

Conclusion

Araniko Highway is a beautiful and important highway in Nepal. The highway provides a vital link between Nepal and China and is an important transportation route for both goods and people. Despite the challenges it faces, Araniko Highway remains an important part of Nepal’s infrastructure and an important symbol of the country’s relationship with China.
