Southern Nc Beaches Map

July 30, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Are you planning to visit the Southern NC beaches but don’t know where to start? Look no further! This article will guide you through the best beaches in Southern NC and provide you with a detailed map to make your trip planning easier.

The Best Southern NC Beaches

Southern NC beaches offer a diverse range of experiences for visitors. From family-friendly beaches to secluded ones, there’s something for everyone. Here are some of the best beaches you should explore:

1. Wrightsville Beach

Wrightsville Beach is known for its crystal-clear waters and wide sandy beaches. It’s a perfect spot for families, as it has a lively atmosphere, and there are plenty of restaurants and shops nearby.

2. Carolina Beach

Carolina Beach is a popular destination for beachgoers, thanks to its boardwalk, amusement park, and aquarium. It’s also a great spot for fishing and surfing.

3. Kure Beach

Kure Beach is known for its quiet and peaceful atmosphere. It’s an ideal spot for those who want to escape the crowds and enjoy some tranquility.

4. Oak Island

Oak Island is a hidden gem, with miles of beaches that are perfect for long walks. It’s also a great place for fishing and boating.

How to Get There

The Southern NC beaches are easily accessible by car, and there are several airports nearby. The closest airport is Wilmington International Airport, which is about 20 miles away from most of the beaches.

Where to Stay

There are plenty of accommodation options near the Southern NC beaches, including hotels, vacation rentals, and campgrounds. If you’re looking for a luxurious stay, there are several high-end resorts in the area.

What to Do

Apart from sunbathing and swimming, there are plenty of activities to do in Southern NC beaches. You can go fishing, surfing, kayaking, or paddleboarding. You can also explore the nearby attractions, such as the North Carolina Aquarium and the Fort Fisher State Historic Site.

Conclusion

Overall, Southern NC beaches offer a fantastic vacation experience for visitors. With this guide and map, you can plan your trip with ease and make the most of your time in this beautiful part of the country. So, pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable beach vacation!
