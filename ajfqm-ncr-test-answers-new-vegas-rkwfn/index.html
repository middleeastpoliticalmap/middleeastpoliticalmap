Introduction

Fallout: New Vegas is a post-apocalyptic action role-playing video game developed by Obsidian Entertainment. It is the fourth major installment in the Fallout series and was released in 2010. This article will explore how to find the NCR test answers in Fallout: New Vegas.

What is the NCR Test?

The NCR Test is a multiple-choice quiz that players can take to join the New California Republic. The test consists of 20 questions and covers topics such as NCR history, politics, and military tactics. To join the NCR, players must answer at least 13 questions correctly.

Where to Take the NCR Test?

Players can take the NCR test at the NCR Correctional Facility, which is located northeast of Primm. The NCR Correctional Facility is a heavily guarded prison that houses dangerous criminals. To access the facility, players must first complete the “My Kind of Town” quest in Primm. Once players have completed the quest, they can travel to the NCR Correctional Facility and take the test.

Finding the NCR test answers can be a bit challenging, as players must explore the game world and talk to various characters to gather information. After taking the NCR test, players will receive a score based on their answers. If players score at least 13 out of 20, they will be able to join the NCR and receive a NCR uniform. If players score lower than 13, they can retake the test after waiting for 24 hours.
