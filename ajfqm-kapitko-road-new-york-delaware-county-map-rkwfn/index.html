Kapitko Road New York Delaware County Map

October 12, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

If you’re looking for a scenic drive through the countryside of Delaware County, New York, Kapitko Road is a must-see. This winding road takes you through rolling hills, lush forests, and charming farmland. But Kapitko Road is more than just a pretty drive. There are plenty of hidden gems to discover along the way, from hiking trails to local farms and wineries. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at Kapitko Road and all it has to offer.

The Route

Kapitko Road is a 6-mile stretch of road that runs from State Route 10 in Stamford to State Route 23 in South Kortright. Along the way, you’ll pass through the towns of Harpersfield and Kortright. The road is mostly paved, but there are some gravel sections, so be prepared if you’re driving a low-clearance vehicle.

Things to See and Do

There are plenty of things to see and do along Kapitko Road. Here are just a few highlights:

1. Harpersfield Covered Bridge

The Harpersfield Covered Bridge is a historic landmark that spans the West Branch of the Delaware River. Built in 1827, it’s one of the oldest covered bridges in the country. You can park at the nearby picnic area and take a short walk to the bridge for some great photo opportunities.

2. Hiking Trails

There are several hiking trails along Kapitko Road, including the Kortright Creek Trail and the Catskill Scenic Trail. These trails offer a chance to explore the natural beauty of the area and get some exercise at the same time.

3. Local Farms and Wineries

Delaware County is known for its farming community, and Kapitko Road is no exception. There are several local farms and wineries along the route, including Betty Acres Farm and Red Barn Farm Winery. Stop in for some fresh produce or a tasting of local wines.

Planning Your Trip

If you’re planning a trip to Kapitko Road, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Check the Weather

The weather can be unpredictable in this part of New York, so be sure to check the forecast before you go. Bring appropriate clothing and gear for the conditions.

2. Pack a Picnic

There are several picnic areas along Kapitko Road, so pack a lunch and enjoy a scenic meal with a view.

3. Respect Private Property

Some of the land along Kapitko Road is privately owned, so be sure to respect any posted signs and stay on public property.

Conclusion

Kapitko Road is a hidden gem in Delaware County, New York. Whether you’re looking for a scenic drive or some outdoor adventure, there’s something for everyone along this route. So pack your bags, hit the road, and discover all that Kapitko Road has to offer.
