Introduction

India is a vast country with diverse cultures and languages. Telugu is one of the major languages spoken in South India. Many people in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana states speak Telugu. In this article, we will discuss the India map with states in Telugu.

What is Telugu?

Telugu is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian states of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and some parts of Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Odisha. It is the second most spoken language in India after Hindi.

India Map with States in Telugu

The India map with states in Telugu shows the 28 states and 9 union territories of India. The states in Telugu are called ‘raajyaalu’ and the union territories are called ‘saamstaanikaalu.’ The map is useful for Telugu-speaking people who want to learn about the different states and their locations.

Andhra Pradesh and Telangana

Andhra Pradesh and Telangana are two states in South India where Telugu is the official language. Andhra Pradesh is known for its rich history and culture, while Telangana is known for its IT industry and Hyderabad city.

Other States

There are 26 other states in India, including Maharashtra, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Punjab, and West Bengal. Each state has its own unique culture, language, and traditions.

Union Territories

India has 9 union territories, including Delhi, Chandigarh, Puducherry, and Andaman and Nicobar Islands. These territories are directly governed by the Central government of India.

Importance of Knowing India Map with States in Telugu

Knowing the India map with states in Telugu is important for Telugu-speaking people who want to learn about the different states and their cultures. It also helps in understanding the political and geographical boundaries of India.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the India map with states in Telugu is a useful resource for Telugu-speaking people who want to learn about the different states and their locations. It is important to know the India map with states in Telugu for better understanding of the country’s diverse cultures and traditions.
