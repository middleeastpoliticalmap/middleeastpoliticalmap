Mexico City Dangerous Areas Map

August 6, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Mexico City, the capital of Mexico, is a vibrant city with rich culture and history. However, like any other big city, there are certain areas that are considered dangerous for tourists. It is important for tourists to be aware of these areas to ensure their safety during their visit. In this article, we will provide a comprehensive guide to Mexico City’s dangerous areas map.

Why is it important to know?

Mexico City has a high crime rate, and tourists are often targeted by criminals. Knowing the dangerous areas in the city can help tourists avoid these areas and reduce the risk of becoming a victim of crime. It is essential to be aware of the dangers to enjoy a safe and enjoyable trip to Mexico City.

North Zone

The North Zone of Mexico City is considered one of the most dangerous areas for tourists. This area includes the neighborhoods of Tepito and La Lagunilla, which are known for their high crime rate. Tourists are advised to avoid these areas, especially at night.

East Zone

The Eastern Zone of Mexico City is also considered dangerous for tourists. This area includes the neighborhoods of Iztapalapa and Neza, which are known for their high crime rate. Tourists are advised to avoid these areas, especially at night.

South Zone

The South Zone of Mexico City is relatively safer than the other zones. However, there are still some areas that tourists should avoid. These areas include the neighborhoods of Tlalpan and Xochimilco, which are known for their high crime rate.

West Zone

The West Zone of Mexico City is considered the safest zone for tourists. This area includes the neighborhoods of Santa Fe and Bosques de las Lomas, which are known for their high-end shops and restaurants. Tourists can feel safe walking around these areas, even at night.

Tips for staying safe in Mexico City

It is important for tourists to take precautions to stay safe while in Mexico City. Here are some tips to keep in mind: – Avoid walking alone at night. – Only use authorized taxis or ride-sharing services. – Keep valuables out of sight. – Stay alert and aware of your surroundings. – Don’t carry large amounts of cash with you. – Always carry a form of identification with you.

Conclusion

Mexico City is a beautiful city with a rich culture, but it is important for tourists to be aware of the dangers and take precautions to stay safe. By familiarizing themselves with Mexico City’s dangerous areas map and following the tips provided in this article, tourists can enjoy a safe and memorable trip to Mexico City.
