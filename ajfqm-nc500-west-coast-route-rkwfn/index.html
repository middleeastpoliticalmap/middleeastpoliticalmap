Nc500 West Coast Route

December 14, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

If you are looking for a thrilling adventure in 2023, then the NC500 West Coast Route should definitely be on your bucket list. This scenic route is a 500-mile circular drive that takes you through some of the most stunning landscapes in Scotland. From rugged coastlines to majestic mountains, the NC500 has something for everyone. In this article, we will take a closer look at this route and share some tips to help you make the most of your journey.

Why Choose the NC500 West Coast Route?

The NC500 West Coast Route is a popular choice for travelers who are seeking an escape from the hustle and bustle of modern life. This route takes you through some of the most remote and scenic parts of Scotland, allowing you to experience the natural beauty of the country. Whether you are a nature lover, a history buff, or an adventure seeker, the NC500 has something to offer.

Things to See and Do

One of the best things about the NC500 is that there are so many things to see and do along the way. Here are just a few of the highlights:

1. Dunrobin Castle: This stunning castle is located near Golspie and is one of the most popular attractions on the route. The castle dates back to the 13th century and is home to a collection of fine art, antique furniture, and porcelain.

2. Smoo Cave: This unique cave is located near Durness and features a combination of sea and freshwater. The cave is accessible by boat and is a popular spot for photographers.

3. Cape Wrath: This remote headland is located at the northwestern tip of Scotland and is only accessible by foot or boat. The area is home to a variety of wildlife, including seals, dolphins, and whales.

4. Whaligoe Steps: These historic steps are located near Wick and were used by fishermen to access the rocky shoreline. The steps are now a popular spot for visitors who want to take in the stunning views.

When to Visit

The best time to visit the NC500 West Coast Route is during the summer months, from June to September. During this time, the weather is mild and the days are long, allowing you to explore the route at your own pace. However, it is important to remember that the route can be busy during peak season, so be prepared for crowds.

How to Prepare

Before embarking on your NC500 adventure, it is important to make sure that you are prepared. Here are a few tips to help you get ready:

1. Plan your route: Take the time to plan your route and decide on the places that you want to visit along the way.

2. Pack for the weather: Scotland’s weather can be unpredictable, so make sure that you pack for all conditions.

3. Rent a car: The best way to explore the NC500 is by car, so make sure that you rent a vehicle before you go.

4. Book accommodation in advance: During peak season, accommodation can be hard to find, so make sure that you book in advance.

Conclusion

The NC500 West Coast Route is a must-see destination for anyone who loves adventure and natural beauty. From historic castles to rugged coastlines, this route has something for everyone. By following our tips, you can make the most of your journey and create memories that will last a lifetime. So, what are you waiting for? Start planning your NC500 adventure today!
