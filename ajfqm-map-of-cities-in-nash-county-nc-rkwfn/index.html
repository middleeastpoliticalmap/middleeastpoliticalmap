Rocky Mount

Rocky Mount is the largest city in Nash County and is located in the northeastern part of North Carolina. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse community. Some of the top attractions in Rocky Mount include the Rocky Mount Mills, the Imperial Centre for the Arts and Sciences, and the Rocky Mount Children’s Museum and Science Center.

Nashville is a small town located in Nash County, NC. It is known for its quiet, peaceful atmosphere and friendly people. Some of the top attractions in Nashville include the Nash Arts Center, the Nashville Chamber Music Series, and the Nashville Blooming Festival.

Spring Hope
