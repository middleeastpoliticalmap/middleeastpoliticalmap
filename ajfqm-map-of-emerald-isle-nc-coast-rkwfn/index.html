Map Of Emerald Isle Nc Coast

February 18, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Emerald Isle is a beautiful coastal town located in North Carolina. It is a popular vacation spot for tourists who love to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea. The town is known for its crystal clear waters, white sandy beaches, and breathtaking views. This article will explore the map of Emerald Isle NC Coast, highlighting some of the top attractions, activities, and landmarks.

Location and Directions

Emerald Isle is located in Carteret County, North Carolina. It is situated between two major cities, Morehead City and Jacksonville. To get to Emerald Isle, visitors can take Highway 58 or Highway 24. Both routes offer scenic views of the coast and take you directly to the town.

Top Attractions

Emerald Isle has plenty of attractions for visitors to enjoy. One of the top attractions is the Bogue Inlet Fishing Pier, which is the longest fishing pier on the east coast. Visitors can enjoy fishing, sightseeing, and watching the sunset. Another popular attraction is the Emerald Isle Woods Park, which is a nature preserve that offers hiking trails, picnic areas, and scenic views of the coast.

Activities and Landmarks

In addition to attractions, Emerald Isle also has plenty of activities and landmarks to explore. Visitors can enjoy kayaking, paddleboarding, and surfing in the crystal clear waters. They can also visit landmarks like the Point, which is a popular spot for watching dolphins and enjoying the sunset.

Beaches

Emerald Isle is known for its beautiful beaches, which are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing. The town has five public beach access points, each offering a unique experience. Visitors can choose from the Eastern Regional Beach Access, the Western Regional Beach Access, the Ocean Drive Beach Access, the Islander Drive Beach Access, and the Emerald Isle Public Beach Access.

Restaurants and Shopping

Emerald Isle has plenty of restaurants and shopping options for visitors. Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood, local cuisine, and international dishes at restaurants like Rucker John’s, Crab Shack, and Flipperz Family Bar and Grill. They can also shop for souvenirs, clothing, and gifts at stores like Emerald Isle Beach Gear and Island Harbor Marina.

Accommodations

Emerald Isle has plenty of accommodations for visitors, ranging from hotels and motels to vacation rentals and campsites. Visitors can choose from popular options like the Islander Hotel and Resort, the Oceanview Inn, and the Emerald Isle Realty Vacation Rentals.

Events and Festivals

Emerald Isle hosts several events and festivals throughout the year, offering visitors the opportunity to experience the town’s culture and traditions. Some of the popular events include the Emerald Isle St. Patrick’s Festival, the Emerald Isle Marathon, and the Beach Music Festival.

Conclusion

Emerald Isle is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves the sun, sand, and sea. From its beautiful beaches and crystal clear waters to its top attractions and activities, there is something for everyone in this coastal town. So, grab a map of Emerald Isle NC Coast and start planning your next vacation today!
