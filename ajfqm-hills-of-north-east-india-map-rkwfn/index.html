Hills Of North East India Map

September 3, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

If you are looking for an adventure in 2023, then the hills of North East India should be on your list. The region is known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and mouth-watering cuisine. With its serene beauty and tranquillity, the hills of North East India are the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

The Map

The hills of North East India are spread across seven states and are a part of the Eastern Himalayas. The map of the region is dotted with various peaks, valleys, and rivers that offer a unique experience to travellers. Some of the most famous hill stations in the region include Darjeeling, Gangtok, Shillong, and Tawang.

The Beauty of North East India

The hills of North East India are famous for their stunning beauty. The region is blessed with lush green forests, snow-capped mountains, and crystal clear lakes. The hill stations are also home to various exotic flora and fauna that are unique to the region.

The hills of North East India are also culturally rich. The region is home to various tribes, each with their own unique culture and traditions. Travellers can experience the cultural diversity of the region by visiting the various festivals and events that take place throughout the year.

Things to Do

Travellers can engage in various activities in the hills of North East India. The region is perfect for trekking, camping, and hiking. The hills are also home to various adventure sports like paragliding, river rafting, and mountain biking.

Travellers can also visit the various monasteries that are scattered across the region. The monasteries offer a glimpse into the rich cultural and religious heritage of the region.

Food

The hills of North East India are also famous for their cuisine. The region is known for its delicious momos, thukpa, and various other delicacies. Travellers can also enjoy the local brew, including the famous Darjeeling tea.

How to Reach

The hills of North East India are well-connected to the rest of the country. Travellers can reach the region by air, rail, or road. The nearest airport is Bagdogra, which is well-connected to major cities in India. The region is also well-connected by rail, with major railway stations in New Jalpaiguri and Guwahati.

Conclusion

The hills of North East India are a must-visit destination in 2023. The region offers a unique experience to travellers with its stunning beauty, rich culture, and mouth-watering cuisine. So pack your bags and head to the hills of North East India for an adventure of a lifetime.
