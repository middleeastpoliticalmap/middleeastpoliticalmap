Introduction

Mounds State Park is a beautiful park located in Anderson, Indiana. The park boasts of several unique features including ten earthwork mounds built by the Adena-Hopewell people between 160 B.C. and 200 A.D. To get the most out of your visit to Mounds State Park, it’s essential to have a map that guides you through the park. In this article, we’ll explore the Mounds State Park Indiana map and how it can help you make the most of your visit.

Getting to Mounds State Park

Before we dive into the Mounds State Park Indiana map, let’s first discuss how to get to the park. Mounds State Park is located in Anderson, Indiana, and is easily accessible from I-69. From I-69, take exit 226 and follow the signs to the park entrance. Once you get to the park, you’ll need a map to navigate the park’s trails, attractions, and facilities.

The Mounds State Park Indiana Map

The Mounds State Park Indiana map is a comprehensive guide that contains all the information you need to navigate the park. The map features all the park’s trails, facilities, and attractions, including the Adena-Hopewell mounds. The map is available at the park’s entrance and is also downloadable from the park’s official website.

Trails

Facilities

Attractions

Tips for Using the Mounds State Park Indiana Map

To get the most out of the Mounds State Park Indiana map, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Familiarize yourself with the map

Before you start your hike or visit any of the park’s attractions, take a few minutes to familiarize yourself with the map. This will help you plan your visit and ensure that you don’t miss any of the park’s highlights.

2. Pay attention to the trail difficulty level

The Mounds State Park Indiana map is an essential tool that will help you make the most of your visit to the park.
