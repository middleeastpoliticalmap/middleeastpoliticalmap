Map Of Indianapolis Hospitals

August 26, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Indianapolis, the capital city of Indiana, is home to numerous hospitals and healthcare facilities that cater to the needs of its residents. Keeping track of these healthcare facilities can be challenging, especially for those who are new to the city. In this article, we will provide you with a map of Indianapolis hospitals that will help you navigate through the city with ease.

Why is it important to know the location of hospitals in Indianapolis?

Accidents and emergencies can happen at any time, and it is crucial to know the location of the nearest hospital. Knowing the location of hospitals in Indianapolis can save you time and help you get the medical attention you need as soon as possible. It can also be helpful for those who are planning to move to Indianapolis and want to ensure that they have easy access to healthcare facilities.

Map of Indianapolis Hospitals

Below is a map of Indianapolis hospitals. The map shows the location of hospitals and healthcare facilities in the city.

1. St. Vincent Hospital

St. Vincent Hospital is a regional healthcare provider with a Level I Trauma Center. The hospital is located at 2001 W 86th St, Indianapolis, IN 46260. It has over 800 beds and offers a wide range of medical services, including cardiology, neurology, oncology, and women’s health.

2. Indiana University Health Methodist Hospital

Indiana University Health Methodist Hospital is a teaching hospital located at 1701 N Senate Blvd, Indianapolis, IN 46202. It has over 1,100 beds and is a Level I Trauma Center. The hospital offers services such as cancer care, neurology, orthopedics, and transplant services.

3. Eskenazi Health

Eskenazi Health is a public health system that serves the residents of Marion County. The hospital is located at 720 Eskenazi Ave, Indianapolis, IN 46202. Eskenazi Health has over 300 beds and offers services such as primary care, behavioral health, and women’s health.

4. Community Hospital East

Community Hospital East is located at 1500 N Ritter Ave, Indianapolis, IN 46219. It is a community hospital with over 200 beds that offers services such as cancer care, cardiology, and orthopedics.

5. Community Hospital North

Community Hospital North is located at 7150 Clearvista Dr, Indianapolis, IN 46256. It is a community hospital with over 200 beds that offers services such as cancer care, cardiology, and neurology.

Conclusion

Knowing the location of hospitals in Indianapolis can be helpful in emergencies and can save you time in finding the medical attention you need. The map and information provided in this article can help you navigate through the city and make informed decisions about your healthcare needs.
