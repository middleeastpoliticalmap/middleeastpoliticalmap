Road Map Of South Island New Zealand

October 24, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

If you are planning to visit New Zealand in 2023, the South Island is a must-visit destination. With its stunning landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, and snow-capped mountains, the South Island is a paradise for nature lovers. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive road map of the South Island, highlighting the must-visit places and hidden gems.

Christchurch

Your South Island journey will most likely start from Christchurch, the largest city in the South Island. Christchurch is known for its vibrant arts scene, beautiful gardens, and Gothic architecture. Don’t miss the opportunity to visit the Christchurch Botanic Gardens, which boasts a native New Zealand garden and a conservatory filled with tropical plants.

Akaroa

Located just 75 km southeast of Christchurch, Akaroa is a charming French-inspired town. The town is situated on the Banks Peninsula and is known for its beautiful harbor, stunning views, and unique architecture. Take a stroll along the waterfront and enjoy some of the best seafood in New Zealand.

Tekapo

As you drive further south, you will come across Lake Tekapo, one of the most beautiful lakes in New Zealand. The lake is famous for its turquoise waters and the Church of the Good Shepherd, which is situated on its shore. Tekapo is also an excellent place for stargazing, and you can visit the Mount John Observatory to explore the night sky.

Queenstown

No South Island road trip is complete without a visit to Queenstown, the adventure capital of New Zealand. The town is situated on the shores of Lake Wakatipu and is surrounded by snow-capped mountains. Queenstown is known for its adrenaline-inducing activities such as bungee jumping, skydiving, and jet boating.

Milford Sound

Located in Fiordland National Park, Milford Sound is one of the most visited tourist destinations in New Zealand. The sound is famous for its towering cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and unique wildlife. You can take a boat tour to explore the sound and witness its beauty up close.

Dunedin

Situated on the east coast of the South Island, Dunedin is a charming university town. The town is known for its Scottish heritage, stunning architecture, and beautiful beaches. Don’t miss the opportunity to visit the Otago Peninsula, home to rare wildlife such as penguins and seals.

Abel Tasman National Park

Located at the northern end of the South Island, Abel Tasman National Park is known for its golden beaches, clear waters, and lush forests. You can take a kayak tour to explore the park’s coastline or hike one of the many trails that lead to stunning viewpoints.

Kaikoura

If you love marine life, Kaikoura is a must-visit destination. The town is situated on the east coast of the South Island, and its waters are home to a wide variety of marine life such as whales, dolphins, and seals. You can take a boat tour to witness these magnificent creatures up close.

Conclusion

The South Island of New Zealand is a stunning destination that offers something for everyone. From the vibrant city of Christchurch to the breathtaking Milford Sound, the South Island will leave you in awe. Make sure to add these must-visit destinations to your road map and explore the hidden gems along the way.
