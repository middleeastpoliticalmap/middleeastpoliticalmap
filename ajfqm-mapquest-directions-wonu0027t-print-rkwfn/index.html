Mapquest Directions Won\U0027T Print

August 30, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

MapQuest is one of the most popular online mapping services used worldwide. It provides accurate and reliable driving directions, maps, traffic updates, and other features. However, users may encounter issues while trying to print MapQuest directions. If you’re facing the same problem, don’t worry. In this tutorial, we’ll guide you on how to fix it.

Why Won’t MapQuest Directions Print?

There could be several reasons why MapQuest directions won’t print. Some of the common ones include outdated browser, corrupted printer driver, misconfigured printer settings, or issues with the MapQuest website. Before trying any solution, make sure your printer is properly connected to your device and has sufficient ink and paper.

How to Fix MapQuest Directions Not Printing?

1. Use a Different Browser

If you’re using an outdated or unsupported browser, MapQuest directions may not print correctly. In that case, try using a different browser such as Google Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. Make sure it’s updated to the latest version.

2. Clear Browser Cache and Cookies

Clearing your browser cache and cookies can sometimes resolve printing issues. To do this, go to your browser’s settings or options and select “Clear browsing data.” Choose the time range and the data you want to clear and click on “Clear data.”

3. Disable Ad Blocker

If you’re using an ad blocker, it may interfere with MapQuest’s printing functionality. Try disabling the ad blocker temporarily and see if it solves the issue.

4. Check Printer Settings

Make sure your printer settings are properly configured. Go to your device’s control panel and select “Devices and Printers.” Right-click on your printer and select “Printer properties.” Check the settings for paper type, orientation, and size.

5. Update Printer Driver

If your printer driver is outdated or corrupted, it may cause printing issues. Go to your printer manufacturer’s website and download the latest driver for your printer model.

6. Contact MapQuest Support

If none of the above solutions work, contact MapQuest support for help. They may have additional troubleshooting steps or offer a solution to the issue.

Conclusion

Printed directions can be useful when you’re on the go and don’t have access to a device. However, if MapQuest directions won’t print, it can be frustrating. By following the above solutions, you can troubleshoot and resolve the issue quickly. If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to share in the comments below. Happy printing!
