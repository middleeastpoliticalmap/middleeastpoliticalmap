Minecraft Decimation Mod New York Map

September 7, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Minecraft is a popular sandbox game that allows players to create and explore their own worlds. The game has been around for over a decade and has seen many mods and updates. One of the popular mods is the Decimation Mod, which adds a post-apocalyptic twist to the game. In this article, we will explore the new Decimation Mod map of New York in 2023.

What is the Decimation Mod?

The Decimation Mod is a Minecraft mod that adds a post-apocalyptic theme to the game. It features new weapons, items, and a new game mode. The mod also adds zombies and other creatures to the game, making it more challenging and exciting.

Exploring the New York Map

The new Decimation Mod map of New York takes place in the year 2023. The city has been destroyed by a virus that turned people into zombies. The player must survive in this harsh environment by scavenging for resources and fighting off zombies and other creatures.

Starting Out

When you start the game, you will be dropped into a random location in New York. You will have a few basic items such as a knife, some food, and water. You must explore your surroundings and gather resources such as wood, stone, and metal to build shelter and weapons.

Building Shelter

Building shelter is crucial in the Decimation Mod. You can build a simple shelter with just a few blocks of wood or stone. As you gather more resources, you can build a more complex structure with walls, doors, and windows. Your shelter will protect you from zombies and other creatures at night.

Gathering Resources

There are many resources to gather in the Decimation Mod. You can gather wood by chopping down trees, stone by mining rocks, and metal by scavenging in abandoned buildings. You can also find food and water by hunting animals and searching for water sources.

Fighting Zombies and Other Creatures

Zombies and other creatures are a constant threat in the Decimation Mod. You must be prepared to fight them off with weapons such as guns, knives, and bows. You can also use traps and barricades to slow them down.

Conclusion

The new Decimation Mod map of New York is a challenging and exciting addition to Minecraft. It offers a unique gameplay experience that is different from the vanilla game. Whether you are a fan of Minecraft or just looking for a new game to play, the Decimation Mod is definitely worth checking out.
