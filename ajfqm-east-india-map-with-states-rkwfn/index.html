East India Map With States

March 11, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction:

India is a diverse country with 36 states and union territories. East India is one of the most culturally rich regions of the country, comprising of seven states. The East India map with states is an essential tool for tourists, students, and researchers interested in exploring the region’s beauty and history. In this article, we will discuss the East India map with states and highlight some important facts about each state.

The Seven States in East India:

The seven states in East India are West Bengal, Odisha, Jharkhand, Bihar, Assam, Manipur, and Mizoram. Each state has its unique culture, history, and tourist attractions. Let’s explore each state in detail.

West Bengal:

West Bengal is known as the cultural capital of India and is famous for its literature, music, and art. It is home to the iconic Howrah Bridge, Victoria Memorial, and Sunderbans National Park. Kolkata, the state’s capital, is a bustling city with a rich history of British colonialism.

Odisha:

Odisha is a coastal state and is renowned for its ancient temples, beaches, and wildlife reserves. The state’s capital, Bhubaneswar, is known as the Temple City of India, with more than 700 temples. The Konark Sun Temple, Lingaraj Temple, and Chilika Lake are some of the popular tourist destinations.

Jharkhand:

Jharkhand is a newly formed state and is known for its natural beauty and cultural heritage. The state is home to the famous Betla National Park, Dassam Falls, and Jonha Falls. The capital city, Ranchi, is a peaceful city surrounded by hills and forests.

Bihar:

Bihar is the birthplace of Buddhism and is home to some of the most important Buddhist sites, such as Bodh Gaya, Nalanda, and Rajgir. The state is also famous for its Madhubani paintings, litchi fruit, and silk production. Patna, the state’s capital, is a historical city with a rich past.

Assam:

Assam is a northeastern state known for its tea plantations, wildlife, and culture. Kaziranga National Park, Manas National Park, and Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary are some of the famous wildlife reserves. The state’s capital, Guwahati, is a bustling city with a mix of ancient and modern architecture.

Manipur:

Manipur is a small state with a rich history and culture. The state is famous for its classical dance form, Manipuri, and the Loktak Lake, the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India. Imphal, the state’s capital, is a beautiful city surrounded by hills and valleys.

Mizoram:

Mizoram is a mountainous state and is known for its natural beauty, tribal culture, and handicrafts. The state’s capital, Aizawl, is a picturesque city with a mix of traditional and modern architecture. The state is home to the Murlen National Park, Phawngpui National Park, and the famous Reiek Tlang trek.

Conclusion:

The East India map with states is a fascinating tool that helps people explore the region’s beauty and diversity. Each state has something unique to offer, be it culture, history, or nature. We hope this article has provided you with some valuable insights into the seven states in East India. Plan your trip and experience the beauty of East India in 2023!
