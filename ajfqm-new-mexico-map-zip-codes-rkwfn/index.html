What are Zip Codes?

Zip codes are a system of postal codes used by the United States Postal Service (USPS) since 1963. They are five-digit codes that represent a specific geographic area, such as a city, town, or region. Zip codes are used to identify mail delivery locations and are also used for statistical purposes.

Importance of Zip Codes

Knowing the zip codes of locations within New Mexico is essential for several reasons. Firstly, it helps to ensure that mail is delivered to the correct location. Secondly, it makes it easier to find specific locations when using GPS or other navigation systems. Thirdly, zip codes are used by various businesses and organizations for marketing and demographic research.
