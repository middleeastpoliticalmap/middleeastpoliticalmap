Top Attractions in Rhode Island

Rhode Island is a beautiful state with several attractions. Some of the top attractions in Rhode Island include:

Newport Mansions

Newport Mansions is a collection of historic mansions located in Newport, Rhode Island. These mansions were built in the late 19th century and early 20th century by wealthy families. Today, these mansions are open to the public for tours.

Block Island

Block Island is a small island located off the coast of Rhode Island. It is known for its beautiful beaches and stunning views. You can take a ferry to Block Island and spend a day exploring the island.

Roger Williams Park Zoo

Roger Williams Park Zoo is a popular zoo located in Providence, Rhode Island. It has several exhibits, including elephants, giraffes, and zebras. The zoo also has a botanical garden and a carousel.
