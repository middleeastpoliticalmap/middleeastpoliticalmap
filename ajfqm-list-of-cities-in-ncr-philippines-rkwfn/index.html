List Of Cities In Ncr Philippines

September 19, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

The National Capital Region, also known as Metro Manila, is the capital region of the Philippines. It is composed of 16 cities and one municipality, all of which are located within the boundaries of the region. In this article, we will be discussing the 16 cities in NCR Philippines.

Caloocan City

Caloocan City is located in the north of Manila and is the fourth most populous city in the Philippines. It is known for being the birthplace of Andres Bonifacio, a revolutionary leader of the Philippines.

Las Pinas City

Las Pinas City is located in the south of Manila and is known for its famous Bamboo Organ, a unique musical instrument made entirely of bamboo. It is also home to the Sarao Motors factory, which produces the iconic jeepney.

Makati City

Makati City is the financial center of the Philippines and is home to the Philippine Stock Exchange. It is also known for its upscale shopping malls, restaurants, and nightlife.

Malabon City

Malabon City is located in the north of Manila and is known for its delicious seafood dishes, such as the famous “pancit malabon”. It is also home to the Malabon Zoo, which houses various exotic animals.

Mandaluyong City

Mandaluyong City is located in the east of Manila and is known for its shopping centers, such as the SM Megamall and Shangri-La Plaza. It is also home to the headquarters of major corporations, such as San Miguel Corporation and Globe Telecom.

Manila City

Manila City is the capital city of the Philippines and is known for its historical landmarks, such as Intramuros, Rizal Park, and the Manila Cathedral. It is also home to the University of Santo Tomas, the oldest university in Asia.

Marikina City

Marikina City is located in the east of Manila and is known for its shoe industry. It is also home to the Marikina River Park, which is a popular destination for outdoor activities such as biking and jogging.

Muntinlupa City

Muntinlupa City is located in the south of Manila and is known for being the location of the New Bilibid Prison, the largest prison facility in the Philippines. It is also home to the Alabang Town Center, a popular shopping mall.

Navotas City

Navotas City is located in the north of Manila and is known for its fishing industry. It is also home to the Navotas Centennial Park, which is a popular destination for jogging and cycling.

Paranaque City

Paranaque City is located in the south of Manila and is known for its resorts and casinos, such as the Solaire Resort and Casino and the City of Dreams Manila. It is also home to the Ninoy Aquino International Airport, the main gateway to the Philippines.

Pasay City

Pasay City is located in the south of Manila and is known for its cultural landmarks, such as the Cultural Center of the Philippines and the Philippine International Convention Center. It is also home to the Mall of Asia, one of the largest shopping malls in the world.

Pasig City

Pasig City is located in the east of Manila and is known for its historical landmarks, such as the Pasig Cathedral and the Immaculate Conception Cathedral. It is also home to the Ortigas Center, a major business district in the Philippines.

Quezon City

Quezon City is the largest city in the Philippines and is known for its educational institutions, such as the University of the Philippines Diliman and Ateneo de Manila University. It is also home to major shopping malls, such as the SM City North EDSA and Trinoma.

San Juan City

San Juan City is located in the east of Manila and is known for its traditional Filipino cuisine, such as the famous “sisig”. It is also home to the Greenhills Shopping Center, a popular destination for bargain shopping.

Taguig City

Taguig City is located in the south of Manila and is known for its modern developments, such as the Bonifacio Global City and McKinley Hill. It is also home to the Venice Grand Canal Mall, a popular shopping and dining destination.

Valenzuela City

Valenzuela City is located in the north of Manila and is known for its industrial parks, such as the Valenzuela Industrial Park and the Northgate Cyberzone. It is also home to the Valenzuela People’s Park, which is a popular destination for outdoor activities.

Conclusion

The 16 cities in NCR Philippines offer a diverse range of attractions and experiences. From historical landmarks to modern developments, from traditional cuisine to exotic animals, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Explore the National Capital Region and discover the beauty and richness of the Philippines.
