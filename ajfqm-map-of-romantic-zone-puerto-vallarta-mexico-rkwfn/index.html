Map Of Romantic Zone Puerto Vallarta Mexico

September 12, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Puerto Vallarta is a popular tourist destination located on the west coast of Mexico. The Romantic Zone, also known as Old Vallarta, is a charming area in the city that is perfect for couples and travelers who are looking for a more laid-back and romantic atmosphere. In this article, we will explore the map of the Romantic Zone and discover the best places to visit.

Getting There

The Romantic Zone is located in the southern part of Puerto Vallarta, just a short walk from the city center. You can easily reach the area by taking a taxi, bus, or walking. If you are staying in a nearby hotel, you can also take advantage of their shuttle services.

Exploring the Map

The Romantic Zone is a small area, but it is packed with attractions and things to do. The main street in the area is called Basilio Badillo, and it is lined with restaurants, bars, and shops. You can also find many art galleries and boutique stores in the area.

Restaurants and Bars

One of the best things about the Romantic Zone is the food. There are many restaurants that serve authentic Mexican cuisine, as well as international dishes. Some of the most popular restaurants in the area include El Andariego, La Palapa, and Café des Artistes. There are also many bars that offer live music and a great atmosphere, such as The Red Room and Andale.

Art Galleries and Shops

If you are interested in art and culture, you will love the Romantic Zone. The area is home to many art galleries and shops that sell local handicrafts and souvenirs. Some of the most popular galleries in the area include Galleria Dante, Galeria Pacifico, and Peyote People.

Beaches

The Romantic Zone is also home to some of the best beaches in Puerto Vallarta. Playa Los Muertos is the most popular beach in the area, and it is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and people-watching. There are also many water sports activities that you can enjoy, such as snorkeling and jet skiing.

Nightlife

If you are looking for a vibrant nightlife scene, the Romantic Zone has plenty to offer. There are many bars and clubs in the area that offer live music, dancing, and a great atmosphere. Some of the most popular nightclubs in the area include Mandala, La Vaquita, and Strana.

Conclusion

The Romantic Zone of Puerto Vallarta is a charming and romantic area that is perfect for couples and travelers who are looking for a more laid-back and relaxed atmosphere. The area is packed with attractions, restaurants, and shops that offer something for everyone. Whether you are interested in art and culture, food, or nightlife, the Romantic Zone has it all. So, if you are planning a trip to Puerto Vallarta, don’t forget to visit the Romantic Zone and explore all that it has to offer.
