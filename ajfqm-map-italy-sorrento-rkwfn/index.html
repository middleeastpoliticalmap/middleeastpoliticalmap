Map Italy Sorrento

October 26, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Italy is a country that offers a wide range of experiences to tourists, from the grandeur of Rome to the serene beauty of Sorrento. This charming coastal town is located in the Campania region of Southern Italy and is known for its scenic beauty, delicious food, and rich history. If you’re planning a trip to Italy, Sorrento should definitely be on your itinerary. In this article, we’ll explore the best way to map your trip to Sorrento in Italy.

Getting There

Sorrento is easily accessible from Naples, which has an international airport. You can take a train or a bus from Naples to Sorrento, which takes about an hour. Alternatively, you can also take a ferry from Naples to Sorrento, which is a scenic way to arrive in the town. Once you’re in Sorrento, you can take a taxi or a bus to get around.

Where to Stay

Sorrento has a range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. If you’re looking for a luxurious stay, you can choose from the Grand Hotel Excelsior Vittoria, Bellevue Syrene, or the Hotel Mediterraneo Sorrento. For budget-friendly options, you can choose from Hostel Le Sirene, Casa Dominova, or Hotel Il Nido.

What to See and Do

Sorrento has plenty of attractions to keep you busy during your stay. You can visit the historic town center, which is home to the Piazza Tasso, the main square of Sorrento. You can also visit the Cathedral of Sorrento, which is a beautiful church that dates back to the 15th century. For a scenic view of the town, you can take a walk along the Marina Grande, which is a small fishing village.

If you’re interested in history, you can visit the Museo Correale di Terranova, which is a museum that showcases the history of Sorrento. You can also visit the ruins of the ancient Roman city of Pompeii, which is located nearby. For a relaxing day out, you can visit the natural beauty of the Amalfi Coast, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Where to Eat

Sorrento is known for its delicious food, and you can find plenty of restaurants that serve traditional Italian cuisine. If you’re looking for a fine dining experience, you can visit the Michelin-starred Il Buco Ristorante, which offers a range of Italian and Mediterranean dishes. For a more casual dining experience, you can visit Trattoria da Emilia, which is a family-run restaurant that serves traditional Sorrentine dishes.

When to Visit

The best time to visit Sorrento is from April to June and from September to October, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. July and August are peak tourist season, and the town can get crowded and expensive during this time.

Conclusion

Sorrento is a beautiful town that offers a range of experiences to tourists. From its scenic beauty to its delicious food, Sorrento is a must-visit destination for anyone planning a trip to Italy. By following the tips in this article, you can map your trip to Sorrento and make the most of your visit.
