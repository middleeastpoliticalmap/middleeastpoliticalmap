The Conflict between Ukraine and Russia: A Brief Overview

The ongoing conflict between Ukraine and Russia has been one of the most pressing issues in international politics for the past decade. It started in 2014 when Russia annexed Crimea from Ukraine, triggering a series of events that led to a full-scale war in eastern Ukraine. Despite several peace agreements, the conflict has yet to be resolved, and tensions remain high between the two countries.
