Puerto Vallarta Mex Map

February 6, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

Are you planning a trip to Mexico anytime soon? If yes, then you must include Puerto Vallarta in your travel itinerary. Known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage, Puerto Vallarta is a must-visit destination for any travel enthusiast. In this article, we will guide you through the map of Puerto Vallarta, Mexico, and give you all the necessary information you need to plan your trip.

Location and Geography

Puerto Vallarta is a beautiful coastal city located on the Pacific coast of Mexico, in the state of Jalisco. The city is surrounded by the Sierra Madre Mountains, which offer breathtaking views and outdoor adventures. The city is divided into two zones: the Old Town and the Hotel Zone. The Old Town is the historic part of the city, while the Hotel Zone is the modern, touristy part, with high-end resorts and hotels.

Getting There

Puerto Vallarta has its own international airport, Gustavo Díaz Ordaz International Airport, which is located just a few miles from the city center. Many airlines offer direct flights to Puerto Vallarta from major cities in the US, Canada, and Europe. You can also reach Puerto Vallarta by bus or car from other cities in Mexico.

Things to Do

Puerto Vallarta has something to offer for everyone. Whether you are looking for a relaxing beach vacation, an adventure-filled trip, or a cultural experience, Puerto Vallarta has it all. Some of the must-do activities in the city include: – Relaxing on the beaches of Playa Los Muertos, Playa Conchas Chinas, and Playa Mismaloya. – Taking a boat tour to the Marietas Islands to see the hidden beach and the diverse marine life. – Exploring the Old Town and its historic landmarks, such as the Church of Our Lady of Guadalupe and the Malecón. – Trying the local cuisine, such as tacos, ceviche, and tequila. – Going on a hiking or zip-lining adventure in the Sierra Madre Mountains.

Where to Stay

Puerto Vallarta has a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury resorts. If you want to stay close to the beach and the nightlife, then the Hotel Zone is the best option for you. If you prefer a quieter and more authentic experience, then the Old Town is the way to go. Some of the best hotels in Puerto Vallarta include: – Grand Velas Riviera Nayarit – Garza Blanca Preserve Resort & Spa – Casa Velas Hotel Boutique – Hotel Mousai

When to Go

The best time to visit Puerto Vallarta is from November to April when the weather is dry and pleasant. However, this is also the peak tourist season, so expect higher prices and more crowds. If you want to avoid the crowds and get better deals, then consider visiting in May or October. The rainy season runs from June to September, but the rainfall is usually brief and does not affect the overall experience.

Conclusion

Puerto Vallarta is a beautiful and vibrant city that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern amenities. With this guide, we hope that you have all the information you need to plan your trip to Puerto Vallarta, Mexico, and make the most out of your vacation. Don’t forget to explore the city’s map and discover all the hidden gems it has to offer.
