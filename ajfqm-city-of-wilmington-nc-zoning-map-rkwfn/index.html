City Of Wilmington Nc Zoning Map

February 25, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

If you own property in Wilmington, NC, it’s essential to understand the city’s zoning map. Zoning codes dictate how you can use your property, what you can build, and how you can modify your property. This article aims to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the City of Wilmington NC Zoning Map.

What is a Zoning Map?

A zoning map is a visual representation of the city’s zoning codes. It divides the city into different zones, with each zone having specific regulations that determine how the property can be used. The City of Wilmington NC Zoning Map divides the city into six different zones: Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Institutional, Open Space, and Special Purpose.

Residential Zone

The Residential Zone is the most common zone in Wilmington, NC. It includes single-family homes, multi-family homes, and mobile homes. The zoning codes in this zone dictate the size and height of buildings, the number of units in multi-family dwellings, and the setbacks from the property line.

Commercial Zone

The Commercial Zone includes businesses, offices, and retail stores. The zoning codes in this zone dictate the size and height of buildings, the number of parking spaces required, and the types of businesses allowed.

Industrial Zone

The Industrial Zone is for manufacturing, distribution, and storage facilities. The zoning codes in this zone dictate the size and height of buildings, the setbacks from the property line, and the types of activities allowed.

Institutional Zone

The Institutional Zone includes schools, hospitals, and government buildings. The zoning codes in this zone dictate the size and height of buildings, the setbacks from the property line, and the types of activities allowed.

Open Space Zone

The Open Space Zone is for parks, golf courses, and other recreational areas. The zoning codes in this zone dictate the types of activities allowed and the amount of open space required.

Special Purpose Zone

The Special Purpose Zone is for unique properties that don’t fit into the other zones. The zoning codes in this zone dictate the types of activities allowed and the size and height of buildings.

How to Use the Zoning Map

To understand the zoning regulations for your property, you need to find your property on the City of Wilmington NC Zoning Map. You can access the map online or at City Hall. Once you find your property, you can determine the zoning code for your property and the regulations that apply to your property.

Conclusion

Understanding the City of Wilmington NC Zoning Map is essential for property owners. It ensures that you comply with the city’s regulations and can use your property to its fullest potential. If you have any questions about the zoning regulations for your property, contact the City of Wilmington Planning Department for assistance.
