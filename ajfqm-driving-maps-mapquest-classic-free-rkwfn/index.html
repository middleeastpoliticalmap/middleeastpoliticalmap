Driving Maps Mapquest Classic Free

July 19, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

In this day and age, it’s hard to imagine getting lost while driving. With the help of GPS-enabled devices and map apps, finding our way from point A to point B has become a breeze. One of the earliest and most reliable map apps is MapQuest. In this article, we’ll show you how to use MapQuest Classic for free to plan your route, avoid traffic, and discover new places.

What is MapQuest Classic?

MapQuest Classic is the original version of the MapQuest website that was launched in 1996. It allows users to search for locations, get driving directions, and view maps. Unlike the current version of MapQuest, which has a cluttered interface and lots of ads, MapQuest Classic has a clean and simple interface that’s easy to use.

Getting Started with MapQuest Classic

To use MapQuest Classic, go to the website and type in your destination address in the “From” and “To” fields. You can also add waypoints by clicking on the “Add Destination” button. Once you’ve entered your locations, click on the “Get Directions” button to see the route.

Customizing Your Route

MapQuest Classic allows you to customize your route in a number of ways. You can choose the fastest or shortest route, avoid tolls or highways, and even add stops along the way. To customize your route, click on the “Options” button and select your preferences.

Avoiding Traffic

One of the best features of MapQuest Classic is its ability to show you real-time traffic conditions. To view traffic, click on the “Traffic” button on the map. Red lines indicate heavy traffic, yellow lines indicate moderate traffic, and green lines indicate light traffic. You can also view traffic cameras by clicking on the “Cameras” button.

Discovering New Places

MapQuest Classic allows you to explore new places by showing you points of interest along your route. To view points of interest, click on the “Points of Interest” button on the map. You can search for restaurants, hotels, gas stations, and more. Click on a point of interest to see its address, phone number, and website.

Printing Your Directions

If you prefer to have a printed copy of your directions, MapQuest Classic allows you to print them out. To print your directions, click on the “Print” button on the map. You can choose to print a summary or detailed directions, and even customize the font size.

Conclusion

In conclusion, MapQuest Classic is a simple and reliable map app that’s still useful in 2023. It allows you to customize your route, avoid traffic, and discover new places. Best of all, it’s completely free to use. So next time you need to get directions, give MapQuest Classic a try.
