Eastern New York State Counties Map

October 10, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

New York State is known for its bustling city life, but the eastern side of the state boasts a different type of beauty. With 14 counties in the region, there is no shortage of outdoor activities, historical landmarks, and breathtaking scenery to explore. In this article, we will take a closer look at the Eastern New York State Counties Map, highlighting some of the must-visit destinations in the area.

The Counties

The Eastern New York State region is made up of 14 counties, including Albany, Columbia, Dutchess, Greene, Orange, Putnam, Rensselaer, Rockland, Saratoga, Schenectady, Schoharie, Sullivan, Ulster, and Washington. Each county has its unique attractions, making it difficult to choose which ones to visit first.

Outdoor Activities

Outdoor enthusiasts will be pleased with the range of activities available in the Eastern New York State region. From hiking and camping in the Catskill Mountains to fishing and boating on the Hudson River, there is something for everyone. The region is also home to several state parks, including Minnewaska State Park and Harriman State Park, where visitors can enjoy picnicking, swimming, and rock climbing.

Historical Landmarks

History buffs will appreciate the region’s rich past, with several historical landmarks to explore. The Hudson River Valley is home to several estates, including the Vanderbilt Mansion and the Roosevelt Estate. Visitors can also explore the Olana State Historic Site, home to the famous artist Frederic Edwin Church.

Cultural Attractions

The Eastern New York State region is also home to several cultural attractions, including the Woodstock Playhouse and the Bardavon 1869 Opera House. The region is also known for its wineries, with several vineyards offering wine tastings and tours.

Food and Drink

Foodies will be delighted with the range of culinary options available in the Eastern New York State region. The area is known for its farm-to-table restaurants, serving up fresh and locally sourced ingredients. Visitors can also explore the region’s craft breweries and distilleries, sampling some of the finest beers and spirits in the area.

Accommodations

There are several accommodations available in the Eastern New York State region, ranging from budget-friendly motels to luxurious resorts. Visitors can choose to stay in a cozy bed and breakfast, a rustic cabin in the woods, or a modern hotel in the city.

Getting Around

The best way to explore the Eastern New York State region is by car, as many of the attractions are spread out across the 14 counties. Visitors can also take advantage of the region’s public transportation system, including buses and trains.

Conclusion

The Eastern New York State Counties Map is a treasure trove of outdoor activities, historical landmarks, cultural attractions, and delicious food and drink. With 14 counties to explore, visitors will never be bored in this beautiful region of New York State. So, pack your bags, grab your map, and start planning your next adventure in Eastern New York State!
