Venice, Italy – A City of Canals and Narrow Streets

Venice is a city of romance, art, history, and culture. The city is famous for its canals, narrow streets, and beautiful architecture. The city is built on a group of 118 small islands separated by canals and connected by bridges. Venice is one of the most beautiful and unique cities in the world, and exploring it on foot is a must for anyone visiting the city. However, navigating the streets of Venice can be tricky, especially for first-time visitors.
