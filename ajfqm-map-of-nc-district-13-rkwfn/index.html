The Location of NC District 13

NC District 13 is located in the central part of North Carolina, covering parts of Guilford, Randolph, Davidson, and Rowan counties. The district has a diverse population, including urban and rural areas. It is also home to several colleges and universities, such as High Point University and Davidson College.
