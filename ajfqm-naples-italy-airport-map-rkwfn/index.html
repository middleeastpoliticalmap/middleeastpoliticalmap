Naples Italy Airport Map

July 18, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

If you are planning a trip to Naples, Italy, then one of the most important things that you need to know about is the airport map. The Naples International Airport is the gateway to the city and the surrounding area, and understanding the layout of the airport can make your journey much smoother. In this guide, we will explore everything you need to know about the Naples Italy airport map in 2023.

Getting to the Airport

The Naples International Airport is located about 7 km northeast of the city center. There are several ways to get to the airport, including by taxi, bus, or rental car. If you are coming from the city center, then the easiest way to get to the airport is by taxi. However, if you are on a budget, then you can take the ALIBUS shuttle service, which costs only a few euros.

Arrivals Area

Once you arrive at the airport, you will find yourself in the arrivals area. This is where you will go through passport control and collect your luggage. If you need to exchange currency, then there are several exchange booths in this area. There are also ATMs and a post office if you need to send any mail.

Departures Area

The departures area is located on the upper level of the terminal building. This is where you will check in for your flight and go through security. There are several shops and restaurants in this area, so you can grab a bite to eat or do some last-minute shopping before your flight.

Terminal Building

The terminal building at Naples International Airport is divided into two levels. The lower level is where you will find the arrivals area, while the upper level is where you will find the departures area. There are several amenities in the terminal building, including free Wi-Fi, a children’s play area, and a VIP lounge.

Terminal 1 and Terminal 2

Naples International Airport has two terminals: Terminal 1 and Terminal 2. Terminal 1 is the main terminal and handles most of the flights. Terminal 2 is used for low-cost airlines and charter flights. If you are not sure which terminal your flight is departing from, then check with your airline.

Parking

If you are driving to the airport, then you can park your car in one of the several parking areas. There are both short-term and long-term parking options available. If you are picking up or dropping off passengers, then there is a designated area for this purpose.

Transportation Options

If you need to get from the airport to your hotel or other destination, then there are several transportation options available. You can take a taxi, hire a car, or take public transportation. The ALIBUS shuttle service is a popular option for getting from the airport to the city center.

Conclusion

Understanding the Naples Italy airport map is essential if you are traveling to the city. By knowing the layout of the airport, you can make your journey much smoother and avoid any unnecessary stress. We hope that this guide has been helpful in preparing you for your trip to Naples, Italy in 2023.
