Nc State Fair Food Map

August 30, 2022 admin 0 Comments

Introduction

The North Carolina State Fair is one of the most popular events of the year for foodies. With a wide variety of vendors and food options, it can be overwhelming to decide where to start. That’s why we’ve created this guide to help you navigate the fair and find the best eats.

The Best Vendors

One of the highlights of the fair is the food vendors. Some of the must-try vendors this year include the BBQ Barn, which serves up delicious pulled pork sandwiches, and the Deep Fried Everything booth, which is known for its unique fried creations like deep-fried Oreos and Twinkies.

BBQ Barn

The BBQ Barn is a staple at the NC State Fair, and for good reason. Their pulled pork sandwiches are slow-cooked to perfection and served on a soft bun. They also offer a variety of sauces to choose from, including a tangy vinegar-based sauce and a sweet and smoky sauce.

Deep Fried Everything

If you’re looking for something a little more adventurous, the Deep Fried Everything booth is the place to go. They offer deep-fried Oreos, Twinkies, and even pickles. While it may not be the healthiest option, it’s definitely worth trying at least once.

The Best Dishes

In addition to the vendors, there are also specific dishes that are a must-try at the fair. Some of our favorites include the turkey legs, funnel cakes, and fried mac and cheese.

Turkey Legs

The turkey legs at the fair are massive and delicious. They’re slow-roasted over an open flame and seasoned to perfection. It’s the perfect savory snack to enjoy while walking around the fairgrounds.

Funnel Cakes

No trip to the fair is complete without a funnel cake. These deep-fried doughnuts are covered in powdered sugar and are the perfect sweet treat to enjoy. They’re also easy to share with friends and family.

Fried Mac and Cheese

If you’re a fan of mac and cheese, you have to try the fried mac and cheese at the fair. These bite-sized nuggets are breaded and deep-fried until crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside. It’s the ultimate comfort food.

Tips for Navigating the Fair

With so much to see and do at the fair, it can be overwhelming to figure out where to start. Here are a few tips to help you navigate the fairgrounds:

Get There Early

The fair can get crowded, especially on the weekends. Getting there early will give you a chance to beat the crowds and enjoy the fair at a leisurely pace.

Plan Ahead

With so many vendors and food options, it’s a good idea to plan ahead and decide which booths you want to visit. This will help you avoid getting overwhelmed and missing out on some of the best eats.

Stay Hydrated

Walking around the fairgrounds can be tiring, especially on a hot day. Be sure to drink plenty of water and stay hydrated throughout the day.

Conclusion

The NC State Fair is a food lover’s paradise, and with this guide, you’ll be able to navigate the fairgrounds like a pro. Whether you’re in the mood for savory BBQ or sweet funnel cakes, there’s something for everyone at the fair. So grab your friends and family and enjoy all that the fair has to offer!
