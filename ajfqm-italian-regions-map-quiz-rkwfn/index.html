Italian Regions Map Quiz

April 7, 2023 admin 0 Comments

Introduction:

Italy, with its stunning coastlines, rich history, and famous cuisine, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. But how well do you know the country’s regions? Take this map quiz to find out!

The Italian Regions:

Italy is divided into 20 regions, each with its own unique character, culture, and history. The regions are further subdivided into provinces, which are then divided into municipalities. The regions are: 1. Abruzzo 2. Basilicata 3. Calabria 4. Campania 5. Emilia-Romagna 6. Friuli-Venezia Giulia 7. Lazio 8. Liguria 9. Lombardy 10. Marche 11. Molise 12. Piedmont 13. Puglia 14. Sardinia 15. Sicily 16. Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol 17. Tuscany 18. Umbria 19. Aosta Valley 20. Veneto

The Map Quiz:

Now, let’s see how well you know the Italian regions! Take a look at the map below and try to identify as many regions as you can. Don’t worry if you don’t get them all on the first try – it’s not easy!

Quiz Answers:

1. Abruzzo 2. Basilicata 3. Calabria 4. Campania 5. Emilia-Romagna 6. Friuli-Venezia Giulia 7. Lazio 8. Liguria 9. Lombardy 10. Marche 11. Molise 12. Piedmont 13. Puglia 14. Sardinia 15. Sicily 16. Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol 17. Tuscany 18. Umbria 19. Aosta Valley 20. Veneto

Why Take the Quiz?

Taking this quiz is a fun and interactive way to test your knowledge of the Italian regions. Not only will you learn more about the country’s geography, but you’ll also be able to impress your friends and family with your newfound knowledge!

Conclusion:

Italy is a fascinating country with a rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse regions. Taking this map quiz is a great way to explore the regions and learn more about what makes each one unique. So, how well did you do? Let us know in the comments below!
